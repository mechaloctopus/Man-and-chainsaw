Meet Josh: The Man Behind the Chainsaw

Hey, I'm Josh. I'm a simple man, and while I don't claim to know everything about trees, I definitely know how to cut timber safely and efficiently.

With over 15 years of experience, including 4 critical years serving with the U.S. Forest Service as a Type 2 and Type 1 Wildland Firefighter on engine and hand crews, I've handled tough situations and learned the right way to bring trees down.

My focus is on getting your unwanted or hazardous trees on the ground safely, without the overhead costs of bigger companies.

What I Do (And What I Don't)

My specialty is the safe and efficient removal of trees. If you have a tree that needs to come down, whether it's dead, hazardous, or just in the way, I'm your guy.

I'm not an arborist or an expert in tree health diagnostics. My skill lies in felling and removal. If you need complex pruning for tree health or disease treatment, an arborist is your best bet. But if you need a tree safely on the ground, I can handle it.

I primarily use reliable Stihl chainsaws to get the job done right.
